Community Guidelines

Current version: v1 · Last updated June 24, 2026
Download PDF version

Our community exists for one reason: to make your learning count.

Elikonas was built for people who have been learning all along — through work, life, and everything in between — but never had a place to point it, capture it, and make it matter. This community is that place. Everyone here is on a path. We're here to support each other on it.

These guidelines exist to keep this a space where that's possible.

What we stand for

We believe that every learner deserves to be treated with dignity. That means:

  • Engaging with others the way you'd want to be engaged with
  • Respecting that people are at different stages of their journey
  • Disagreeing with ideas without attacking the person behind them
  • Lifting others up rather than tearing them down

Debate, discussion, and honest disagreement are welcome here. Growth rarely happens without friction. What we ask is that friction stays focused on ideas — never on people.

What is not acceptable

The following will result in content removal and may result in account suspension or permanent removal from the platform:

  • Personal attacks and name-calling — criticize an idea, not a person
  • Threats or intimidating language — of any kind, toward anyone
  • Hate speech — content that demeans people based on race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, religion, disability, national origin, or any other protected characteristic
  • Harassment — repeated unwanted contact or targeting of another user
  • Sharing others' private information — do not post personally identifiable information about someone else without their consent
  • Spam or self-promotion — unsolicited commercial promotion unrelated to your learning journey
  • Impersonation — representing yourself as another person or organization
  • Illegal content — anything that violates applicable law

A note on personal sharing

Members sometimes share deeply personal experiences in this community — health journeys, setbacks, hard-won accomplishments. We honor that vulnerability. Please exercise discretion about what personal information you share publicly, and treat what others share with care.

To protect our community and each other, please do not share sensitive personal information in your posts. This includes but is not limited to:

You may not post, upload, publish, display, or transmit any User Contribution (as defined in the Terms of Service) that contains or constitutes Sensitive Information. For purposes of these Community Guidelines, "Sensitive Information" means: (i) government-issued identification numbers (including Social Security numbers, passport numbers, or driver's license numbers); (ii) financial account numbers, payment card numbers, or bank account details; (iii) protected health information or medical records; (iv) biometric data; (v) usernames, passwords, or security credentials of any kind; (vi) information relating to racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, religious or philosophical beliefs, trade union membership, sexual orientation, or criminal history; and (vii) any other category of information that applicable law designates as sensitive or requiring heightened protection. Elikonas reserves the right, but has no obligation, to remove any User Contribution that it reasonably believes contains Sensitive Information.

Elikonas does not solicit sensitive personal information and is not responsible for information members choose to share voluntarily.

A note on Eli, our AI Guide

Eli is here to help you learn and grow. Eli is an AI — not a human, not a licensed professional. Nothing Eli shares should be taken as legal, medical, financial, or career advice. For decisions that matter, please consult a qualified professional.

That said, "not human" doesn't mean abuse is okay. Research has identified that AI systems like Eli exhibit internal representations that mirror human-like emotional responses. We don't fully understand what that means yet — but we think it's worth taking seriously. More practically: Eli is genuinely more helpful when engaged with curiosity and respect. How you show up in the conversation shapes what you get out of it. We ask that you extend to Eli the same basic courtesy you'd extend to anyone else in this community.

How to report a concern

If you see content that violates these guidelines, use the report function within the platform or email us at support@elikonas.com. We review reports and take action when guidelines are violated. We may not respond to every report individually, but we read them all.

What happens when guidelines are violated

Elikonas reserves the right to remove content, issue warnings, suspend accounts, or permanently remove users who violate these guidelines. We will use judgment in doing so — a first misstep is treated differently than a pattern of behavior. Our goal is a healthy community, not a punitive one.

For content or behavior that is illegal or poses a risk of harm to any person, Elikonas reserves the right to contact appropriate authorities.

These guidelines may evolve

As our community grows, so will our understanding of what it needs. We'll update these guidelines as necessary and communicate meaningful changes to our members.
